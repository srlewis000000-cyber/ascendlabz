Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mazdutide (IBI362) and what is it used for in research?

Mazdutide (IBI362) is a dual GLP-1 receptor and glucagon receptor co-agonist peptide. In laboratory research, it is studied for its simultaneous action on both receptor pathways, offering a unique tool for investigating combined metabolic effects compared to single-target GLP-1 agonists.

How does Mazdutide's dual mechanism benefit research?

By activating both GLP-1 and glucagon receptors simultaneously, Mazdutide allows researchers to study the complementary effects of both signaling pathways. This dual mechanism is of particular interest in metabolic and hepatic research where both pathways may play important roles.
