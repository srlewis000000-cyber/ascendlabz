Storage, Handling & Reconstitution

Storage: Both lyophilized peptides are stable at -20°C. After reconstitution, refrigerate at 2-8°C and use within 30 days. Mix only at time of use. Lyophilized peptides should be kept in original sealed packaging until use to prevent moisture uptake.

Reconstitution: Bring the lyophilized vial to room temperature before opening. Reconstitute with bacteriostatic water (each vial reconstituted separately) by injecting solvent down the inner wall of the vial; do not direct the stream onto the lyophilized cake. Gently swirl — do not vortex or shake — until fully dissolved. Allow 5-10 minutes for complete solvation.

Handling: Use sterile technique throughout. Wipe vial septa with isopropyl alcohol before each draw. Document reconstitution date, solvent volume, and resulting concentration on the vial label for traceability.
