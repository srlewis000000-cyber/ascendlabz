Frequently Asked Questions

What receptors does Melanotan 2 target in research?

Melanotan 2 is a non-selective agonist of the melanocortin receptor family, binding to MC1R through MC5R. Research applications include studying pigmentation pathways, energy homeostasis, immune function, and reproductive biology mediated by these receptor subtypes.

How does MT-2 compare to Melanotan 1?

Melanotan 2 is a cyclic heptapeptide with broader receptor affinity compared to the linear Melanotan 1 (afamelanotide). MT-2 binds to MC1R, MC3R, MC4R, and MC5R, making it a useful research tool for studying multiple melanocortin pathways simultaneously.

What is the recommended storage for MT-2?

Melanotan 2 lyophilized powder should be stored at -20°C, protected from light and moisture. Under these conditions, it maintains stability for up to 24 months. All AscendLabz products are shipped with cold pack to preserve integrity during transit.
