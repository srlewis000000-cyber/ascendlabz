What is the difference between Sermorelin and Ipamorelin?

Sermorelin is a GHRH (Growth Hormone Releasing Hormone) analogue while Ipamorelin is a GHRP (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide). They work via different receptor pathways and are often studied in combination in research settings.

Is Sermorelin the same as GHRH?

Sermorelin is a synthetic fragment of GHRH containing the first 29 amino acids (GHRH 1-29). It mimics the biological activity of endogenous GHRH in research models and binds to the same GHRH receptor.
