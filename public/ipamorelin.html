Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Ipamorelin unique among GHRP peptides?

Ipamorelin is known for its selectivity — it stimulates growth hormone release without significantly increasing cortisol or prolactin in research models, unlike older GHRPs. This selective profile makes it a valuable research tool for studying GH axis function.

How does Ipamorelin differ from GHRP-2 and GHRP-6?

Ipamorelin has a more selective action profile compared to GHRP-2 and GHRP-6. Research indicates it produces fewer off-target effects related to appetite stimulation and cortisol release, making it useful for studying isolated GH secretion pathways.
