Frequently Asked Questions

What is AOD-9604 and how is it used in research?

AOD-9604 is a synthetic peptide representing the C-terminal fragment (amino acids 176-191) of human growth hormone. In laboratory research, it is studied for its effects on lipolysis, adipose tissue metabolism, and fat cell differentiation without the growth-promoting effects of full-length hGH.

How does AOD-9604 differ from full-length HGH?

AOD-9604 is specifically the lipolytic region of hGH. Unlike full-length growth hormone, research indicates that AOD-9604 does not significantly stimulate IGF-1 production or affect blood glucose levels, making it useful for studying isolated fat metabolism mechanisms.
