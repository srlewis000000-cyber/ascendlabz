Frequently Asked Questions

What is GHRP-2 and how does it work in research?

GHRP-2 (Growth Hormone Releasing Peptide-2) is a synthetic hexapeptide that acts as a potent agonist at the ghrelin receptor (GHS-R1a). In laboratory research, it stimulates GH release from the pituitary and is used to study the GH secretagogue system, appetite signaling, and metabolic regulation.

How does GHRP-2 compare to GHRP-6 and Ipamorelin?

GHRP-2 produces a strong GH pulse in research models with moderate effects on cortisol and prolactin release. Compared to GHRP-6, it has less appetite-stimulating effect. Compared to Ipamorelin, it has broader receptor activity and typically produces a stronger GH response, making it useful for studying GH axis physiology.

What is the storage requirement for GHRP-2?

GHRP-2 lyophilized powder should be stored at -20°C, protected from light and moisture. Under these conditions it remains stable for 24+ months. AscendLabz ships all peptides in appropriate packaging to maintain integrity during transit.
