Storage, Handling & Reconstitution

Reconstitute with bacteriostatic water by injecting solvent down the inner wall of the vial; do not direct the stream onto the lyophilized cake. Gently swirl — do not vortex or shake — until fully dissolved. Allow 5-10 minutes for complete solvation.

Handling: Use sterile technique throughout. Wipe vial septa with isopropyl alcohol before each draw. Document reconstitution date, solvent volume, and resulting concentration on the vial label for traceability.
