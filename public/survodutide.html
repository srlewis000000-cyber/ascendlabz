What is Survodutide (BI 456906) used for in research?

Survodutide is a dual GLP-1 receptor and glucagon receptor agonist being researched for its potential effects on metabolic function, body composition, and liver health. It is used exclusively for in vitro and laboratory research purposes.

How does Survodutide compare to other GLP-1 agonists?

Unlike single-target GLP-1 agonists, Survodutide activates both GLP-1 and glucagon receptors simultaneously. This dual mechanism makes it a uniquely interesting research compound for scientists studying energy expenditure and metabolic regulation.
