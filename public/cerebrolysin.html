Frequently Asked Questions

What is Cerebrolysin used for in research?

Cerebrolysin is a neuropeptide mixture studied for its neurotrophic and neuroprotective activities. Research applications include investigating neuroplasticity mechanisms, BDNF-like signaling pathways, synaptic plasticity, and neuroprotective mechanisms in neuronal cell culture and animal model studies.

What active peptides does Cerebrolysin contain?

Cerebrolysin contains a complex mixture of low-molecular-weight peptides including fragments with activity at BDNF, NGF, and CNTF receptors. Identified components include peptides from the FPE peptide family that have been studied independently for their neurotrophic activities.
