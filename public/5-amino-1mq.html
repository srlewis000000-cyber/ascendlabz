Frequently Asked Questions

What is 5-Amino-1MQ used for in research?

5-Amino-1MQ is a potent, cell-permeable inhibitor of NNMT (Nicotinamide N-methyltransferase). Research focuses on its ability to influence NAD+ metabolism, adipocyte differentiation pathways, and metabolic regulation in laboratory cell and animal model studies.

How does NNMT inhibition relate to NAD+ research?

NNMT consumes methyl groups from SAM (S-adenosyl methionine) and is a regulator of NAD+ precursor metabolism. By inhibiting NNMT, 5-Amino-1MQ is studied for its effects on cellular NAD+ availability, methylation balance, and downstream metabolic signaling pathways.
